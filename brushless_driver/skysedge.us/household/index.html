Window-Mounted Sun Mirror

There's so much untapped potential in using light as part of a home or office beautification strategy. This is a simple suction cup-mounted articulating mirror to reflect light from a sunny window into a dark recess of a room.

But that's what window prism are for, you say? Yes, those are good too, but if you just want to distribute more sunlight into a room, a mirror is much more effective.
