RoboWheel™ Bracket


spacer

A bracket to easily mount the RoboWheel to a horizontal surface. M6 clearance holes match the RoboWheel bolt pattern. Print your own or order one here printed from high-quality carbon-filled PETG. This is a tough, impact-resistant material that should hold up for many years of service. NOTE: The photo above shows the right-hand version.
